Our Story

The explosive growth in the availability of computer devices in our societies around the World even in some of the poorest, most remote communities was due to IT professionals focusing on technological advancement.But a team of unique software professionals noticed that as more and more people buy and use these devices they also potentially bring software hazards with them depending on how they are used.The team shifted its focus on dealing with these software problems plaguing community members.On this shift,in 2020 a free online software consultancy platform was born,M-softsln(Mashinani Software Solutions)
